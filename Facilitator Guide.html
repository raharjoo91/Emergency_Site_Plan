Objectives

  1. Design a compliant 2D site layout for an emergency field hospital based on a selected scenario (Type 1, 2, or 3).
  2. Apply minimum technical standards for functional zones, WASH, and safety buffers.
  3. Integrate essential services: command, reproductive health, and mental health (MHPSS) where required.

Site Planning Requirements

All layouts must include:

  1. Site boundary with dimensions
  2. Six functional zones: Clinical, Support, Logistics, Administration, Staff, Security
  3. Patient entry point (front)
  4. Ambulance bay
  5. Clinical tents with bed capacity
  6. Water tank (15) + purifier (14)
  7. Sanitation ‚â•30m from water
  8. Medical waste ‚â•500m from population
  9. Utility corridors
  10. Staff accommodation with separate WASH
  11. Emergency exits
  12. Parking/loading area
  13. Security perimeter with flag (19)
